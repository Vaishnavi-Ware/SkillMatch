About SkillMatch

SkillMatch is India's dedicated platform for connecting blue-collar workers, domestic helpers, and skilled tradespeople with families, businesses, and organisations who need them. We believe every worker deserves dignity, opportunity, and fair pay.

🏛️

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between skilled workers and employers in India — making employment accessible, transparent, and fair for everyone.

✅

Verified Profiles

Every worker on SkillMatch goes through an ID verification process, giving employers peace of mind and workers a trusted digital identity.

🌍

Pan-India Reach

From metros to tier-2 cities, SkillMatch operates across India covering all states and union territories for maximum reach.

🤝

Fair Wages

We actively promote salary transparency and fair wages based on local market rates so workers get what they deserve.

📱

Mobile First

Designed for India's mobile-first population — works great even on basic smartphones with low internet speeds.

🔒

Safe & Private

Worker personal data is protected. Contact sharing happens only when both parties agree to connect.
